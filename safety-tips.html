Safety Tips

Your safety is our priority. Follow these guidelines to protect yourself when searching for or listing properties on ISHFADI.

Quick Safety Checklist

👥 For Seekers (Buyers)
📍

Meeting Safely

  • Always meet in public places for initial discussions
  • Bring a friend or family member when viewing properties
  • Schedule viewings during daylight hours
  • Share your location and itinerary with someone you trust
💬

Communication Tips

  • Keep communication within the ISHFADI platform initially
  • Be wary of hosts who push to communicate off-platform quickly
  • Ask detailed questions about the property and neighborhood
  • Request video calls before in-person meetings if possible
💳

Payment Safety

  • Never pay before viewing the property in person
  • Avoid wire transfers to unknown accounts
  • Get all agreements in writing before making payments
  • Be suspicious of deals that seem too good to be true
🏠 For Hosts (Sellers)
📸

Listing Safely

  • Remove personal items and valuables from listing photos
  • Don't include your exact address in the public listing
  • Use ISHFADI's messaging system for initial contact
📞

Conducting Viewings

  • Screen potential seekers before sharing your address
  • Have someone else present during viewings
  • Keep viewings during daylight hours when possible
  • Trust your instincts — decline if something feels wrong

🚨 Red Flags to Watch For

⚠ Pressure to pay or decide immediately
⚠ Refusal to meet in person or video call
⚠ Prices significantly below market rate
⚠ Requests for unusual payment methods
⚠ Vague or inconsistent property details
⚠ Claims of being overseas or unavailable

Report Suspicious Activity

If you encounter suspicious behavior or believe you've been targeted by a scam, please report it immediately. Your report helps keep our community safe.

Report to safety@ishfadi.com
