Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner. Choose whether you want to find a place (seeker) or list a place (host), then fill in your details including name, email, password, and location. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
Seekers are users looking for accommodation — they can browse listings, save favorites, and contact hosts. Hosts are property owners or managers who can list their properties, manage inquiries, and connect with potential tenants or buyers.
Yes! You can have both roles on the same account. Access your dashboard settings to enable the additional role. Many users are both seekers in one location and hosts in another.
ISHFADI is a global platform available in over 50 countries. During signup, you can select your country and city to see listings in your area.
Go to your Seller Dashboard and click "Add Listing". Fill in the property details including title, description, location, price, property type, and upload photos. Once submitted, your listing will be reviewed before going live.
Verified listings have been reviewed by our team to confirm the property exists and the information is accurate. Verification may include document checks, photo verification, or property visits in some regions.
Most listings are verified within 24-48 hours. Complex verifications requiring additional documentation may take up to 5 business days.

Basic listings are free. We offer Featured Listings for hosts who want increased visibility — these appear at the top of search results and include a "Featured" badge.
Featured Listings get priority placement in search results, appear on the homepage, and include a special badge. This increases your listing's visibility and helps you find tenants or buyers faster.
