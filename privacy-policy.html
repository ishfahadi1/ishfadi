Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

👁️ Introduction

At ISHFADI, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform. We are committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring transparency in how we handle it.

🗄️ Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Location data (country and city)
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Listing Information

  • Property details and descriptions
  • Photos and media you upload
  • Pricing and availability information

Usage Data

  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Search queries and preferences

✅ How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our platform services
  • To connect seekers with hosts and facilitate communication
  • To verify listings and maintain platform integrity
  • To send important notifications and updates
  • To improve our services based on usage patterns
  • To comply with legal obligations

🔒 Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure authentication mechanisms

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Access

Request a copy of your personal data

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request deletion of your account and data

Portability

Export your data in a common format
